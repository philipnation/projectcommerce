About us

Welcome to venormall, where we empower businesses to create their own fully functional e-commerce applications with ease. Our platform offers a unique multitenancy solution, allowing each user to have their own dedicated subdomain on our website. With our innovative technology, entrepreneurs and businesses can quickly launch their online stores, complete with powerful management features for products, orders, reports, and more. We believe in simplifying the e-commerce experience while providing robust functionality to help businesses thrive in the digital world.

Key features

  • Custom Subdomain: Every user gets a dedicated subdomain on our platform, enabling them to have their own unique website link (e.g., yourstore.venormall.com). This ensures a professional online presence and enhances branding.
  • Easy App Creation: Our intuitive interface and user-friendly tools enable users to create their own e-commerce app effortlessly. No coding or technical expertise required. Simply select from a range of customizable templates, add your branding elements, and launch your store in no time.
  • Product Management: Seamlessly manage your product catalog with our comprehensive set of tools. Add, edit, categorize, and organize your products, set pricing and inventory levels, and showcase enticing product descriptions and images.
  • Order Management: Keep track of your orders efficiently. View and process orders, manage order status, generate invoices, handle refunds, and ensure timely fulfillment. Our system provides a seamless workflow to streamline your operations.
  • Reports and Analytics: Gain valuable insights into your store's performance with our robust reporting and analytics features. Monitor sales, track customer behavior, analyze trends, and make data-driven decisions to optimize your business strategies.
  • Store Customization: Personalize your e-commerce app to reflect your brand identity. Customize the design, layout, colors, and logo to create a visually appealing and professional storefront that resonates with your target audience.
  • Secure and Reliable: We prioritize the security and reliability of our platform. Rest assured that your data, transactions, and customer information are protected with industry-standard security measures, ensuring a safe online shopping experience.

At venormall, we are dedicated to providing an all-in-one solution for entrepreneurs and businesses to establish their online presence and succeed in the competitive e-commerce landscape. Join us today and unleash your entrepreneurial potential.
